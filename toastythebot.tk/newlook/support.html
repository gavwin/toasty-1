About Us
This area is editable and gives you the opportunity to go into more detail about your business, what you do and what sets you apart from the competition.
 
You should also consider adding key information such as: 
 
  • Areas covered by your business
  • How many years experience you have 
  • Your qualifications
  • Your areas of expertise 
  • Your satisfaction guarentees
  • Any additional services you offer 
   
Remember to keep your wording friendly, approachable and easy to understand…as if you were talking to your customers.
  
You can edit text on your website by double clicking on the text box on your website, alternatively when you select the text box a settings menu will appear, selecting Edit Text from this menu will also allow you to edit the text on your website.
 
If you have any questions, please visit our Support Center where we have lots of helpful articles that will assist you in creating the website of your dreams!
